Instructions

Thanks for visiting this test site! To test the page, just tap the 'start' button. If your phone meows when you shake it, it's working. If there's an error, instructions will follow.

Compatibility

Compatible with iOS and Android. Older models and devices that have not been recently updated may not be supported. Recommended browsers are Safari and Chrome. (Please note that a bug in the version of Safari included with iOS 13.4 prevents the "shake" mode of interactivity from functioning; updating to any other version of iOS fixes the issue.)
